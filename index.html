Blu’s Hacks 2026

Our 7th annual hackathon will take place on Sunday, March 29th, in the Branham High Cafeteria. Middle and high school students of all skill levels are welcome to participate. The event will feature educational workshops in the morning, along with a dedicated Beginner's prize category. Attendees will be provided with lunch and dinner throughout the day. The best submissions will have the chance to win thousands of dollars in prizes. Thanks to the support of our generous sponsors, this event is completely free!

Sign Up ASAP!
